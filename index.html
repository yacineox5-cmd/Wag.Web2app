Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upload the app to Google Play?

Yes, absolutely! The package includes an AAB (Android App Bundle) file that is ready for Google Play Store submission. You'll receive all the necessary files and can upload your app directly to the Play Store.

Is the app compatible with all Android devices?

Yes, the WebView app is compatible with all modern Android devices running Android 5.0 (API level 21) and above, which covers the vast majority of Android devices in use today.

What if my website updates?

Since the app uses WebView technology, any updates you make to your website will automatically reflect in the app. There's no need to update the app itself - your website changes will be visible immediately to app users.

Can I request modifications later?

Yes, you can request modifications! The package includes 10 days of support after delivery. For modifications beyond that period, additional support packages are available. Contact me to discuss your specific needs.

How fast is delivery?

Delivery is fast! You'll receive your complete Android app package (APK and AAB files) within 1-3 days after you provide your website URL and any required branding materials.
